Who Are We?

We are a friendly group of drone enthusiasts and professionals, of all ages and backgrounds, based around Forest Hill in south-east London.

We meet weekly to fly drones, throughout the year (weather permitting) and host regular social events and day trips out of London. Our membership is open to all.
